Home

AlienVault is a developer of commercial and open source solutions to manage cyber attacks, including the Open Threat Exchange, the world's largest crowd-sourced computer-security platform with more than 26,000 participants in 140 countries that share more than one million potential threats daily. Alienvault can be used on your local machine to monitor your virtual machines. It can also be on a much larger scale. Such as on a network that will monitor all the assets on that network for security or other significant events.

Purpose

This HTML page will be used to assist others in setting up Alienvault on their own device. As well as being able to assist in any problems during the installation of Alienvault.

This page will also be used to assist others in setting up Alienvault on the ACM Network. As well as being able to assist in any problems during the installation of Alienvault, assistance with configuring the Alienvault web application, and examples of how to detect and log events happening on the network
